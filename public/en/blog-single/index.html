Al-Tawfiq Consultative Hospital Continues Its Medical Camp for Diabetic Patients.

Naeem Al-RahmanNovember 15, 2023
03 Comments33 Thousand Views

Al-Tawfiq Consultative Hospital continues to organize its free medical camp for diabetic patients, providing specialized care and services to patients across Yemen. The camp aims to provide free medical examinations and provide advice and awareness about diabetes and its prevention and complications.

The camp includes:

  • Free medical examinations: including measuring blood sugar levels, blood pressure checks, cholesterol measurements, and medical consultations by specialists.
  • Educational lectures: about diabetes, its prevention, and complications, with a focus on the importance of proper nutrition and regular exercise.
  • Interactive workshops: on how to deal with diabetes in daily life, managing blood sugar levels, and using insulin correctly.

Dr. ..., a diabetes consultant at Al-Tawfiq Consultative Hospital, stated: "We are proud to organize this medical camp for diabetic patients, believing in the importance of providing specialized care and services to patients across Yemen. Through this camp, we aim to raise awareness among patients about diabetes, its prevention, and complications, and promote awareness of the importance of adopting a healthy lifestyle to maintain their health."

The camp has received great interest from patients, benefiting many of them.

This camp is organized as part of Al-Tawfiq Consultative Hospital's commitment to providing the best medical services to the community and promoting health awareness about various diseases.
